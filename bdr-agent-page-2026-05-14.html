Knowledge
Define what your agent can and cannot discuss with prospects.
Question
What types of commercial insurance do you offer?
Answer
We offer comprehensive commercial insurance including general liability, property, workers' compensation, professional liability (E&O), and commercial auto coverage. Each policy is customized to your business needs.
Question
How long does it take to get a quote?
Answer
Most businesses receive an initial quote within 24 hours. Our AI-powered underwriting allows us to process applications faster than traditional insurers. Complex policies may take up to 3 business days.
Question
What is the minimum company size you work with?
Answer
We work with businesses of all sizes, but our commercial policies are designed for companies with at least 5 employees. For smaller businesses, we can recommend our small business starter package.
Question
Do you offer bundled coverage discounts?
Answer
Yes, we offer a Business Owner's Policy (BOP) that bundles general liability and property insurance at a 15-20% discount compared to purchasing separately. Additional coverages can be added to the bundle.

Objections
Define how your agent responds to common objections during calls.
Objection
"We already have insurance coverage."
Response
That's great that you're already covered! Many of our clients come to us for a second opinion on their coverage. Would it be helpful if I walked you through a quick comparison to make sure you're not overpaying or missing any critical coverage areas?
Objection
"I'm not the right person to talk to about this."
Response
I completely understand. Could you help me connect with the person who handles insurance decisions for your company? I want to make sure the right person has the information they need.
Objection
"Can you just send me an email instead?"
Response
Absolutely, I'd be happy to send over some information. Before I do, could I ask just two quick questions so I can customize what I send? That way you'll get exactly what's relevant to your business rather than generic materials.
Objection
"We're not interested right now."
Response
I appreciate your honesty. Just to make sure I'm reaching out at a better time -- when does your current policy come up for renewal? I can follow up closer to that date so you have options to compare.
